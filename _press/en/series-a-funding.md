# PT X Secures $5M Series A Funding

**Bangkok, Thailand – November 15, 2024** – PT X, a leading innovator in enterprise AI solutions, today announced it has raised $5 million in Series A funding. The round was led by TechVentures Asia, with participation from existing investors.

The new funding will be used to accelerate product development, expand the engineering team, and establish a stronger presence in key Southeast Asian markets.

"We are thrilled to have the support of TechVentures Asia as we scale our mission to democratize AI for businesses across the region," said the CEO of PT X. "This investment validates our approach and will allow us to deliver even more value to our clients."

PT X's flagship platform helps enterprises automate complex workflows and gain actionable insights from their data using advanced machine learning algorithms. The company has seen 300% year-over-year growth and serves clients in finance, retail, and logistics.
