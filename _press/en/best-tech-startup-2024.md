# PT X Named "Best Tech Startup 2024"

**Jakarta, Indonesia – October 28, 2024** – PT X is proud to announce that it has been named "Best Tech Startup 2024" at the prestigious Indonesia Tech Awards. This award recognizes the company's outstanding innovation, rapid growth, and significant contribution to the country's technology ecosystem.

"We are incredibly honored to receive this recognition," said the CTO of PT X. "It is a testament to the hard work and dedication of our entire team, who are passionate about building technology that makes a real difference."

The Indonesia Tech Awards celebrate the best and brightest in the Indonesian technology sector. PT X was selected from a competitive pool of nominees by a panel of industry experts.
