# PT X Wins Best Employer Award

**Bangkok, Thailand – March 5, 2024** – PT X has been recognized as one of the "Best Places to Work" in the technology sector by HR Asia.

The award highlights the company's commitment to employee well-being, professional development, and inclusive culture.
