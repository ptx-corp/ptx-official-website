# PT X Expands to Vietnam

**Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam – June 15, 2024** – PT X is excited to announce the opening of its new office in Ho Chi Minh City, marking a significant milestone in its regional expansion strategy.

The new office will serve as a hub for serving clients in Vietnam and the broader Indochina region.
