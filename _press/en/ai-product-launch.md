# PT X Launches AI Core

**Bangkok, Thailand – April 10, 2024** – PT X today unveiled PT X AI Core, a revolutionary new platform designed to help enterprises build, deploy, and manage AI applications at scale.

The platform offers a suite of tools for data management, model training, and deployment.
