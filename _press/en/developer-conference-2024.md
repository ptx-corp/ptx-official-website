# PT X Hosts Successful Developer Conference 2024

**Bangkok, Thailand – October 12, 2024** – PT X successfully hosted its Annual Developer Conference 2024 today, bringing together over 500 developers, engineers, and tech enthusiasts from across the region.

The one-day event featured keynote speeches from industry leaders, technical workshops, and networking sessions. Topics covered included cloud computing, artificial intelligence, software engineering best practices, and the future of web development.

"Our goal with this conference is to foster a vibrant tech community and provide a platform for learning and collaboration," said the VP of Engineering at PT X. "The energy and enthusiasm we saw today were truly inspiring."
