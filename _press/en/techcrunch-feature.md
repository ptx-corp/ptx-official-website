# PT X Featured in TechCrunch

**San Francisco, USA – July 30, 2024** – PT X was recently featured in a TechCrunch article titled "The Future of Enterprise Software." The piece highlights the company's innovative approach to solving complex business problems and includes an in-depth interview with PT X's CEO.

In the interview, the CEO discusses the company's vision for transforming how businesses leverage technology for growth, the importance of user-centric design in enterprise software, and the role of AI in the future of work.

"It's exciting to see our work recognized on a global stage," said the CEO. "This feature is a great opportunity to share our story and our vision with a wider audience."
