## Project Overview

A comprehensive tool for marketing teams to analyze social media performance and audience demographics.

### Key Features

*   **Sentiment Analysis:** AI-driven sentiment scoring of mentions and comments.
*   **Competitor Tracking:** Monitor competitor activity and benchmarks.
*   **Custom Reports:** Drag-and-drop report builder.

### Technologies Used

*   **Frontend:** React, Apollo Client
*   **Backend:** Node.js, GraphQL
*   **Database:** Neo4j (Graph Database)
