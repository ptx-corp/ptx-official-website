## Project Overview

Connecting patients with healthcare providers through secure video calls and integrated health data monitoring.

### Key Features

*   **Video Consultations:** Secure, high-quality video calls.
*   **Health Data Sync:** Integration with Apple Health and Google Fit.
*   **Prescription Management:** Digital prescriptions and pharmacy routing.

### Technologies Used

*   **Mobile:** Flutter
*   **Backend:** Firebase
*   **Video:** WebRTC
