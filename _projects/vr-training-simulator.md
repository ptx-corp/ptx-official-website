## Project Overview

A VR application designed to train employees on hazardous machinery and safety protocols in a risk-free environment.

### Key Features

*   **Realistic Physics:** Accurate simulation of machinery and interactions.
*   **Scenario Builder:** Instructors can create custom training scenarios.
*   **Performance Metrics:** Detailed tracking of user actions and reaction times.

### Technologies Used

*   **Engine:** Unity 3D
*   **Language:** C#
*   **Hardware:** Oculus / HTC Vive
