## Project Overview

A decentralized application (dApp) ensuring transparency and traceability in the supply chain from manufacturer to consumer.

### Key Features

*   **Immutable Records:** All transactions and movements are recorded on the blockchain.
*   **Smart Contracts:** Automated payments and compliance checks.
*   **Real-time Visibility:** Stakeholders can track goods at every stage.

### Technologies Used

*   **Blockchain:** Ethereum, Solidity
*   **Frontend:** React, Web3.js
*   **Storage:** IPFS
